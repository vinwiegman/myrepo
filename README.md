## Keys analysis of the intro
![image](keytemplatintro.png)
The strongest and most consistent matches appear near the top of the plot, particularly for B major, G# minor, E major, and C# minor. These keys are closely related and share many pitch classes, which is why they appear simultaneously strong. The dominant region is most consistent with B major (or its relative minor G# minor), with brief fluctuations that are most likely because of chord changes within the same key rather than true key changes.
